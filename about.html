A Legacy of Excellence Since 1999

OUR LEGACY

The Origin

Founded in 1999 by the Annu's Educational Society, our institution was built on the core belief of providing exceptional English medium education to the families of Wanaparthy with unwavering discipline.

YOUR FUTURE

The Expansion

What started as a single school has beautifully grown into a multi-campus institution featuring a dedicated residential campus and a specialized Olympiad wing to nurture modern leaders.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Winners of the IMQ National Competition

"At Annu's Academy, we take pride in our students' consistent performance on the national stage. Our bright achievers recently secured multiple Gold and Silver medals along with Certificates of Merit in the IMQ (International Master Quiz)."
