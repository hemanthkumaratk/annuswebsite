EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Heritage & Traditions

Celebrating our roots with vibrant Bathukamma festivities, keeping the rich culture of Telangana alive in our students.

National Pride & Patriotism

Instilling a sense of national identity through grand Independence Day celebrations and patriotic tributes.

Junior Chef & Creative Arts

Exploring creative talents and practical life skills through interactive 'Junior Chef' programs and hands-on activities.

Beyond the Classroom: Journeys of Joy

At Annu's Academy, we believe student life should be full of adventure, laughter, and exploration.

Nehru Zoological Park

Exploring the wonders of the animal kingdom to spark curiosity and wonder, allowing students to connect with nature in a fun, relaxed environment.

Wonderla Water Park

A day of high-energy adventure where students take a break from books to enjoy world-class rides and water slides, fostering friendship and pure happiness.

Ramoji Film City

Stepping into the magic of cinema and grand architecture, where students enjoy a day of spectacular shows and creative inspiration.
